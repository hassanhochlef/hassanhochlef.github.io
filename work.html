Info

An academic project that englobes all women needs in a platform which is built using SpringBoot as a back-end technology and Angular for the front-end side. This Website contains diffrent sections : User profile management, Courses area, Forums, Events area, Jobs opportunities section and also Help & Questions area. Administrators have their own dashboard in order to manage and administrate the website.

The Challenge

Today, many women around the world are victims of discrimination and ignorance even with technological development and the growth of education. Many women do not find the chance to exercise their simple rights to live like men such as work, education and even simple freedom, especially with the "COVID-19" virus threatening the world. Therefore, a simple step forward will do the trick.

The Solution

In this context comes our project aimed at implementing the knowledge acquired during our training within WomenEmpowerment, the objective of which is to develop a platform that aims to regain women's rights around the world. And here comes the idea to make a user friendly platform and a big community for women where they can find and get helped to gain their rights and build their future.
