Info

An academic project where people can gather money, good and items in general to help others, this platform is based on a community of volunteers who seeks to help others and where they know in a transparent way where their donations were distributed.

The Challenge

We can't deny that people fear that their donations goes to the wrong person or aren't well distributed, also the lack of encouragement to donate is increasing during the economic propblems.

The Solution

After a good study of the context, we decided to make this platform which includes associations, individuals, firms and needy people so they can share , help and make events to collect a certain ammount of money and items. Donations will be distributed equally depending on the needs.

Features

Authentification system
Forums section
Like & dislike
